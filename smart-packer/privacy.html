Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Hangzhou Blackstone Dimension Technology Co., Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your data when you use the Smart Packer application (the "App").

Smart Packer is designed as an Offline-First utility. We do not track your location, do not read your contacts, and do not upload your cargo data to any cloud server.

1. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. Here is what we collect (and what we don't):

  • Personal Data (PII): We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • Usage Data: We DO NOT use third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) to track your in-app behavior.
  • Calculation Data: The cargo lists, vehicle parameters, and loading plans you create are processed and stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to us.

2. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Network Access: Strictly used to communicate with the Apple App Store to validate your "Pro Version" license status. We do not use the network to upload user data.
  • File Storage (Files): (Optional) Used only if you explicitly choose to "Export PDF". The App needs this permission to save the generated loading plan PDF to your device's file system (e.g., "On My iPhone" or "iCloud Drive"). We do not access your photos or other private files.

3. Payment Data

Smart Packer operates on a "Freemium" model (Free trial + One-time Purchase).

  • All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple's App Store.
  • We never see, process, or store your credit card information or billing details.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you have full control. If you delete the App, all local data (including history and presets) will be permanently removed from your device.
